About Us

ZRentals is a company that rents out chairs and tables (with or without custom styling), cocktail tables, functional décor & bathrooms!

ZRentals provides custom décor and elevated event essentials designed to create polished, intentional experiences in each occasion. We specialize in custom, luxury-grade rentals that enhance the overall look and flow of weddings, private celebrations, corporate events, and special occasions.

Every setup is approached with attention to detail and a refined aesthetic — ensuring your event feels cared for, cohesive, and professionally executed.
